🔻Characters🔻

🟦Tintin: He is a young, multitalented reporter with a distinctive quiff hairstyle who travels the world with his dog.
🟩Professor Cuthbert Calculus: A character from the Tintin comics. He is a friend of Tintin and is known for his absentmindedness and inventions. Calculus is often depicted as being hard of hearing, leading to misunderstandings and humorous situations.
🟧Snowy: He is a fictional character in The Adventures of Tintin, the comics series by Belgian cartoonist Hergé. Snowy is a white Wire Fox Terrier who is a companion to Tintin, the series' protagonist.
🟥Bianca Castafiore: Bianca Castafiore, nicknamed the "Milanese Nightingale", is a fictional character in The Adventures of Tintin, the comics series by Belgian cartoonist Hergé.
⬜J.W. Müller: Whether sporting a small goatee or fully bearded, under diverse disguises and multiple identities, J.W. Müller has played a part in the biggest events of the twentieth century, always batting for the wrong side.
🟫Rastapopoulos: Genius of evil, enemy of good, great shark swimming in the troubled international waters, he found in Tintin "the impediment to dancing in circles" that never ceases to irritate him.
🟨Rascar Capac:Inca Rascar Capac inspired by the name of Incas who ruled Peru and bearing the names of "Manco-Capac", "Maytac-Capac", or "Huascar".
🟪Captain Archibald Haddock: A character in the comic book series The Adventures of Tintin. He is Tintin's best friend.
